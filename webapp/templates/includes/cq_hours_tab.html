Call Queue Manager

Use this tool to bulk update your Call Queue configurations. You do not need to update the queue wholly. Simply leave a column blank to ignore that specific setting, and only the filled cells will be processed.

Acceptable Hours Formatting:

  • 8:00AM-5:00PM Mon-Fri
  • 9:00AM-12:00PM Mon, 1:00PM-5:00PM Tue-Fri
  • 24/7
