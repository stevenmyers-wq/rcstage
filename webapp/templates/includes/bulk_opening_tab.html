Bulk Opening Hours / Site Management

Instructions & Notes (Bulk Rule Changes):

  • **Prerequisite:** RC Account must be connected.
  • **Function:** Allows mass update of business hours or site-specific call handling rules based on a provided list (e.g., CSV input).
  • **API Endpoint (Sites):** PUT /restapi/v1.0/account/~/sites/<siteId>
  • **API Endpoint (Queues):** PUT /restapi/v1.0/account/~/call-queues/<queueId>/business-hours
  • **Rate Limit:** Low. (Heavy processing, limit consumption to prevent throttling).
  • **Execution Time:** Long (depends on the number of entities; may take minutes).
