Bulk Hours & Rules Management

Mass configuration changes for Sites and Queues business hours and custom rules.

Part 1: Managing Business Hours (In-App Editor)

  1. Click Fetch Site Hours or Fetch Queue Hours to load data.
  2. Use the checkboxes to select rows, then use the "Bulk Edit" panel to apply changes.
  3. The table will update instantly. When finished, click Apply In-App Changes to RingCentral to save.

Part 2: Managing Custom Rules & Backups (CSV Method)

  1. Fetch either Hours or Rules data.
  2. Click Download as CSV. This is your worksheet for rules or your backup file for hours.
  3. Edit the CSV (e.g., set Action to `NEW` for a new rule), then save it.
  4. Upload your modified CSV and click Apply From Uploaded File.
