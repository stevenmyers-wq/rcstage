Automation Speed ⓘ

Automation Speed

Average time from D365 lead creation to the lead being pushed into RingCX. Shows how fast the integration responds to new enquiries.

Power BI: Add a custom date field to D365 (e.g. crd67_rcpusheddts) written by your automation. Use DATEDIFF(SECOND, [createdon], [crd67_rcpusheddts]) and average it.

—
HOT Leads ⓘ

HOT Leads

Leads where the agent or AI dispositioned as HOT (or HOT LEAD). Written back to D365 by the postback webhook after each call.

Power BI: COUNTROWS(FILTER(Leads, LEFT([crd67_rcdisposition], 3) = "HOT"))

—
Conversion Rate ⓘ

Conversion Rate

Percentage of dispositioned leads that came back as HOT. HOT leads ÷ leads with any disposition.

Power BI: DIVIDE([HOT Leads measure], COUNTROWS(FILTER(Leads, [crd67_rcdisposition] <> BLANK())))

—
Dispositions ⓘ

Dispositions Received

Leads that have received a disposition back from RingCX via the postback webhook. Each disposition is written to crd67_rcdisposition in D365.

Power BI: COUNTROWS(FILTER(Leads, [crd67_rcdisposition] <> BLANK()))
