Swap the device on an extension for another existing device — pulled from inventory or from another extension. The number, digital line and emergency address stay on the line and move to the replacement.

Which devices can be swapped? RingCentral can only swap HardPhone and “existing phone” (OtherPhone) devices. An extension whose only device is a RingCentral app / softphone can’t be swapped — those rows will be reported as Failed. Both source and target must already exist on the account; identify them by MAC or device Name.
