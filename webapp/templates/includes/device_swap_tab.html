Device Swap

Swap digital lines and DLs between two existing devices safely.

Instructions & Notes:

  • Prerequisite: RC Account must be connected via Impersonation or the Main Bridge.
  • Function: Swaps the digital line assignments (DLs) between two existing devices (Device A and Device B). This is a multi-step, destructive operation.
  • API Endpoint: Requires sequences of PUT /restapi/v1.0/account/~/device/<deviceId> calls to update the line assignments.
  • Rate Limit: Low/Moderate. Treat as complex provisioning (high chance of hitting 20+ calls/minute limits).
  • Execution Time: Moderate (several seconds for the sequence of calls).
